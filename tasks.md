# Backend

Based on Household Income, we will determine the following:

- Extract federal poverty loans information for government plans to help in medical
- Determine hospital payment plans/assistance

Based on Visit Reason and Diagnosis, we will determine the following:

- Hospital Codes (medical)

Based on the Insurance Plan, we will determine the following:

- In/Out Network status
- Insurance coverage for medical services
- Deductibles and copayments
- Potential issues with insurance claims and reimbursement

Based on the Medical History, we will determine the following:

- Pre-existing conditions that may affect treatment options
- Risk factors for certain medical conditions

Based on the Medication History, we will determine the following:

- Potential drug interactions
- Medication adherence and potential side effects

Based on the Billing Information, we will determine the following:

- Accuracy of billing codes and charges
- Potential issues with insurance claims and reimbursement

Based on the Advance Directives, we will determine the following:

- Potential need for advance care planning and discussions with healthcare providers
